Joe is a craftsman with over 30 years in the field. His hands-on experience encompasses everything from designing and building custom furniture, installing cabinetry and trim, and custom tile work, to renovating entire houses. This experience combined with his solid foundation for craftsmanship and eye for detail allows Joe to consider the full scope of remodeling projects from start to finish.

More

Joe’s management and sales experience is also extensive; from managing the manufacturing, delivery and installation of custom store fixtures and cabinetry, to custom bathrooms and kitchens, he has worked with thousands of clients to create their dreams. Joe holds a BFA in woodworking and furniture design from the University of the Arts in Philadelphia.
